Rafting need-to-knows!

Things to know and expect rafting

Rafting can be a safe and incredibly fun sport if you have everything you need and obey the rules! Children should be ready to follow specific instruction at all times, and we rely on the parent or guardian present with them to determine in advance if they are, the alternative could be risking their safety.

Two kinds of trips, what to bring for each.

Our programs are comprised of a two-day trip, and a half day trip. For the half day trip we provide all food and essentials. However, for the two-day trip, you must bring your own food and snacks in a bag or container, as well as a second days worth of clothing.

What to bring for either or both

For either the half or two day trip, we recommend bringing bathing suits, sunscreen a full-brimmed hat, water bottle, and go-pro with wrist-strap self sticks, we can provide video otherwise. Cellular service is very spotty, and phones will easily get lost, so we recommend turning those in at the start at which time any emergency communications will be provided over the guide satellites messenger.

More than just the thrill!

By following these guidelines on what to bring, and listening to what your guide instructs, we promise to do our best to provide you with the best services possible!
